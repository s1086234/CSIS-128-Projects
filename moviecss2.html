American Sniper

American Sniper is about U.S. Navy Seal Chris Kyle. This film gives viewers a glimpse of Kyle's service through 4 tours in Iraq and toll it took on him as well as his family. Chris Kyle is credited with having the more kills as a sniper than any other American service member, past or present.
